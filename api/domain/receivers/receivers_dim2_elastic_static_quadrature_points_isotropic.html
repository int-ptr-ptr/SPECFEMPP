  • ireceiver – Index of the receiver

  • iseis – Index of the seismogram

  • ispec – Index of the element

  • siesmogram_type – Type of the seismogram

  • xz – Index of the quadrature point

  • isig_step – Seismogram step. Seismograms step = current time step / seismogram sampling rate

  • field – Global wavefield

  • field_dot – Global wavefield time derivative

  • field_dot_dot – Global wavefield second time derivative

  • hprime_xx – Derivates of Lagrange interpolants in the x direction

  • hprime_zz – Derivates of Lagrange interpolants in the z direction
